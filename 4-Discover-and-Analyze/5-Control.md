# 5 · Control

**DMAIC — Control.** Make the gains stick. A discovery project that doesn't hand off to a
sustaining system just decays back to the current state. **Control = the standing program.**

## The control plan
| What we're controlling | Control mechanism | Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Requirements stay current | [Requirements Verification](../3-Requirements/Requirements-Verification-Process.md) re-verification triggers | On contract award/mod; annual |
| People stay qualified | [Continuous-Improvement Loop](../6-Sustain-and-Improve/Continuous-Improvement-Loop.md) (Train→Assess→Document→Audit) | Continuous |
| Certifications stay valid | Expiration tracking + andon on upcoming lapses | Daily/weekly |
| The system keeps working | Internal training audits + CAPA/A3 | Quarterly |
| Leadership stays informed | KPI dashboard + management review | Monthly / annual |

## Standard work = the gains hold
Everything discovered and fixed becomes **standard work**, version-controlled in this repo:
- Training docs, `CERTIFICATIONS.md`, and competency assessments are the standard.
- Changes are tracked (Git) — auditable and reviewable.
- Deviations show up in audits and route to corrective action.

## KPIs carried forward
Certification currency %, training-completion %, first-pass competency rate, time-to-qualify,
open audit findings & closure time, apprentices advancing tiers, retention/fill rates.

## Re-run the loop
DMAIC isn't one-and-done. New contracts, standards revisions, and audit findings re-trigger
**Define/Measure** for the affected area — the discovery approach in this folder is reusable
whenever the scope changes (new subsidiary, acquisition, or contract).

## Control-phase checklist
- ▢ Control plan owned and scheduled
- ▢ Standard work committed to the repo
- ▢ KPI dashboard live and reviewed on cadence
- ▢ Re-verification triggers defined and monitored

**Output:** a sustaining control system — the Initial project's gains are locked into the
standing program.
