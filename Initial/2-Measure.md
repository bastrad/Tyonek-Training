# 2 · Measure

**DMAIC — Measure.** Collect the current-state data objectively. The rule here is *gather, don't
judge* — analysis comes next. Go to where the work happens (**Gemba**) and let the facts speak.

## Data collection plan
| What to measure | Source | Method | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Training records & completion | Records / LMS / HR | Pull & sample for completeness | HR / QA |
| Existing training materials | Shared drives, SMEs | Inventory & date-check | Process owners |
| Certification & expiration status | Trackers / spreadsheets | Compare held vs. required | Training Mgr |
| Systems in use | LMS, HRIS, doc control | Access & walkthrough | IT / QA |
| How training is delivered | Floor observation | Gemba walk + interview | Process owners |
| Contract-flowed requirements | Active contracts | Review with Contracts/PM | Contracts |

## Access & orientation (do this first)
- ▢ Org charts (holding co + each subsidiary)
- ▢ Read access to LMS, shared drives, quality/document-control, HRIS, cert trackers
- ▢ List of active contracts / task orders
- ▢ Recent audit results (internal, customer, DCMA, FAA, ISO/AS registrar, DCSA)

## Intake checklist — documents & data to request
**Program & records**
- ▢ Current training records / matrices (all formats)
- ▢ Existing training materials, work instructions, job aids, lesson plans
- ▢ Certification & expiration tracker(s)
- ▢ Onboarding / new-hire training plans
- ▢ Records-retention policy & storage location

**Systems**
- ▢ LMS content list & usage · ▢ HRIS roster (roles, hire dates) · ▢ Doc-control access

**Compliance & contracts**
- ▢ Contract-flowed training/cert clauses · ▢ Quality manuals referencing training/competence
- ▢ FAA Part 145 approved training program (Services) · ▢ Existing gap analyses / corrective actions

**People**
- ▢ Roster by role/work process · ▢ Current quals/certs held per person (vs. required)

## Interviews — gather what records don't show
Short, consistent conversations per stakeholder (see the map in [Define](1-Define.md)).
| Stakeholder | Opening question |
| --- | --- |
| Subsidiary leadership | "Where does training hurt today, and what gap worries you most?" |
| Quality / QA | "How do you show auditors people are trained? Any recent findings?" |
| Safety / EHS | "Which trainings do you run, how often, and how do you track them?" |
| Security / FSO | "What security training is mandatory, and how is completion tracked?" |
| Human Resources | "Where do records live? What's the onboarding training path?" |
| Contracts / PM | "Which contracts flow specific training or certification requirements?" |
| Process owners / SMEs | "Walk me through how a new person becomes qualified for this task." |

## Measure-phase checklist
- ▢ Data collection plan executed across all subsidiaries
- ▢ Intake checklist items received & logged
- ▢ Gemba walks completed for representative processes
- ▢ Findings logged raw (no conclusions yet); open questions captured

**Output:** a current-state inventory (records, materials, systems, delivery) ready to analyze.
