# Procurement, Kitting & Fabrication — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Procurement, Kitting & Fabrication (SOF Equipment Kits)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **FAR / DFARS** procurement compliance
- **ITAR / EAR** export-control training (defense/SOF equipment)
- Government Property Management — FAR 52.245-1
- Counterfeit parts avoidance (AS5553/AS6081) for electronics
- DOT/IATA HAZMAT shipping (if kits contain hazmat)

## Recommended
- NCMA **CFCM** — Certified Federal Contracts Manager
- ISM **CPSM** / APICS **CPIM**
- Property management (NPMA CPPM)

## Nice-to-have
- Lean / Six Sigma (kitting flow)
- Warehouse / inventory system certification
- Quality (ASQ CQT) cross-training
