# Accounting, Estimating & Purchasing — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** FAR/DCAA-Compliant Accounting, Estimating & Purchasing
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **FAR / DFARS** compliance training
- **Cost Accounting Standards (CAS)** — 48 CFR 9904 awareness
- **DCAA**-compliant accounting system (SF 1408 adequacy) & timekeeping training
- Estimating system compliance — DFARS 252.215-7002
- Contractor Purchasing System Review (**CPSR**) awareness

## Recommended
- NCMA **CFCM / CPCM**
- **CDFM** — Certified Defense Financial Manager (ASMC)
- **CPA** — Certified Public Accountant

## Nice-to-have
- CGFM — Certified Government Financial Manager
- Deltek Costpoint / GovCon ERP certification
- MBA / MSA (accounting)
