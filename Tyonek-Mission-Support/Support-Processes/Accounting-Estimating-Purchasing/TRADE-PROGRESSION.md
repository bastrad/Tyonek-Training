# Accounting, Estimating & Purchasing — Trade Classification & Progression

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** FAR/DCAA-Compliant Accounting, Estimating & Purchasing
**Last updated:**

> Maps this job scope to recognized classifications. Professional/financial occupations with a
> credential-based ladder rather than a classic trade apprenticeship.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Accountant / Cost Analyst (GovCon)
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-2011.00 — Accountants and Auditors
- **Related occupation(s):** 13-1051.00 Cost Estimators; 13-1023.00 Purchasing Agents
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Accounting/Finance apprenticeships exist); credential-based
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-2011.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based (Emerging)

## Progression Path
1. **Accounting/Cost Clerk** — timekeeping, cost coding, FAR/DCAA basics
2. **Cost Accountant / Estimator (CDFM)** — CAS-compliant accounting, estimating-system compliance
3. **Controller / Manager (CPA/CPCM)** — system ownership, DCAA audit interface, mentoring

## Credential Ladder
- Entry: FAR/DFARS + CAS training → Mid: CDFM, NCMA CFCM → Senior: CPA, CPCM
- See `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- DCAA accounting-system adequacy (SF 1408) and CAS are required RTI topics; GovCon ERP (Deltek) skills differentiate.
