# Safeguarding & Storage — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Safeguarding & Storage (Data, Materials, Equipment, Clearances)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- Security clearance (level per contract)
- **NISPOM / 32 CFR Part 117** industrial security training
- **COMSEC** material handling & custodian requirements
- Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) handling & Insider Threat program
- Personnel security administration (SF-86 / e-QIP, DISS) familiarity
- HAZMAT storage (if applicable) — OSHA / EPA RCRA

## Recommended
- **FSO** (Facility Security Officer) certification — CDSE/DCSA
- COMSEC custodian course
- Physical security certification

## Nice-to-have
- NCMS **ISP** — Industrial Security Professional
- ASIS **CPP**
- Special Access Program (SAP) security familiarity
