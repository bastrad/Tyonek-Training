# Special Operations Support — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Special Operations Support
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- Security clearance (typically TS/SCI for USSOCOM programs)
- Deployment readiness — medical screening, CRC processing, immunizations (CONUS/OCONUS)
- OPSEC & Anti-terrorism/Force Protection (Level I) training
- Mission / platform-specific qualification per task order
- CUI / classified material handling

## Recommended
- Prior SOF operational or support experience
- Tactical / weapons familiarization certification (if role requires)
- DOT/IATA HAZMAT shipping (mission equipment)
- Combat lifesaver / TCCC

## Nice-to-have
- Foreign-language proficiency (DLPT)
- SERE training
- Foreign weapons familiarization
