## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Quality Assurance Analyst / Auditor
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 15-1253.00 — Software Quality Assurance Analysts and Testers
- **Related occupation(s):** 51-9061.00 Inspectors/Testers; 13-1041 Compliance Officers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Software QA / Quality apprenticeships exist)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=15-1253.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based (Emerging)

## Progression Path
1. **QA Analyst / Reviewer** — QASP execution, courseware/service review, records
2. **QA Auditor (ASQ CQA)** — internal audits (ISO 9001), CAPA, process compliance
3. **QA Manager / Lead (CMQ/OE)** — quality program ownership, CMMI, mentors analysts

## Credential Ladder
- Entry: ISO 9001 awareness → Mid: ASQ CQA / ISO 9001 Internal Auditor → Senior: ASQ CQE, CMQ/OE
- See `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Run as a certification-based quality career ladder; a competency-based apprenticeship is optional.
