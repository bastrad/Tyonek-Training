## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Instructional Designer / Coordinator
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 25-9031.00 — Instructional Coordinators
- **Related occupation(s):** 13-1151.00 Training & Development Specialists
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Instructional Designer apprenticeships exist)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=25-9031.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based (Emerging)

## Progression Path
1. **Junior Instructional Designer** — ADDIE phases, storyboard, authoring tools, 508 compliance
2. **Instructional Designer (APTD)** — full course/courseware development, SME collaboration
3. **Lead / Senior ISD (CPTD) / Master** — curriculum architecture, evaluation strategy, mentors designers

## Credential Ladder (in lieu of trade journey levels)
- Entry: ADDIE competency → Mid: ATD APTD, authoring-tool certs → Senior: ATD CPTD
- See `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.
