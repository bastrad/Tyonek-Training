# Cyber Range Engineering & Scenario Management — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Cyber Range Engineering & Scenario Management
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required (DoD 8570.01-M / DoD 8140 baseline)
- Security clearance (as required by program)
- **CompTIA Security+ CE** (IAT Level II baseline for privileged access)
- Computing Environment (CE) certification for the range platform
- Annual DoD Cyber Awareness / OPSEC training

## Recommended
- **CCNP / CCNA** — network engineering
- **VMware VCP** / virtualization certification
- Red Hat **RHCSA / RHCE**
- **CEH** (scenario/threat emulation)

## Nice-to-have
- Cyber-range platform vendor certification
- Cloud architecture (AWS/Azure) certification
- **GPEN** (red-team scenario design)
