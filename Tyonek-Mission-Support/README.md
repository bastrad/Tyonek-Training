# Tyonek Mission Support

**Umbrella:** Tyonek
**Legal entity:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Sector:** Mission Support

Tyonek Services Group, Inc. companies provide tailored mission support services for
U.S. Government Cyber Operations, Special Operations, Training and Exercise Support,
Network Security, IT/Software Implementation, Strategic Planning, and Engineering
requirements. Programs are managed and staffed with fully qualified candidates while
maintaining exceptional retention and fill rates and strong customer satisfaction.
Expertise supporting the unique requirements of U.S. Air Force and USSOCOM programs
is built on the extensive experience of key leadership and a commitment to
flexibility and responsiveness to emerging requirements.

## Capabilities
- Training and exercise support
- Tyonek facilities (hosted)
- Government site operations CONUS and OCONUS
- Instructional Systems Design (ISD) and ADDIE-based curriculum and courseware development
- Cyber Range Engineering and Scenario Management
- Software implementation and workforce training
- Licensed storage and safeguarding of required data, materials, equipment, and individual clearances
- Extensive recruiting for hard-to-fill positions
- FAR/DCAA-compliant accounting, estimating, and purchasing systems for fixed-price or cost-type contracts
- Procurement, kitting, and fabrication for specialized equipment kits for special operations requirements
- Extensive leadership experience in U.S. Army and Air Force Special Operations missions (active duty / contractor)

## Certifications (personnel)
- IAT Level III
- GPEN
- CEH
- PMP
- Microsoft
- Other relevant certifications as required

## Work Processes (training folders)

Organized with lean principles: customer-facing mission-delivery service lines are
grouped under `Value-Stream/` (design → deliver → specialized domains); program
enabling functions are grouped under `Support-Processes/`.
