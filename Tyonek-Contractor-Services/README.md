# Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC

**Umbrella:** Tyonek
**Sector:** Construction (General Contracting)

Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC was established in 2017 as an Alaskan limited
liability company and certified as a participant in the Small Business
Administration's 8(a) program in 2019. It is a 100% minority-owned small business.

The company provides general contracting services based in Anchorage, Alaska. As a
unique general building contractor, Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC delivers on
high-demand projects and performs negotiated, design-build projects as well as
private, local, state, and federal public projects. The firm's experienced team
meets challenges in every construction discipline and completes projects across
Alaska and beyond.

## Company Facts
- Established: 2017 (Alaskan LLC)
- SBA 8(a) program participant since 2019
- 100% minority-owned small business
- Based in Anchorage, Alaska

## Project Types
- Negotiated / design-build projects
- Private projects
- Local, state, and federal public projects

## Work Processes (training folders)

Organized with lean principles: the project lifecycle is grouped in flow order under
`Value-Stream/`; functions that run across the whole project are grouped under
`Support-Processes/`.
