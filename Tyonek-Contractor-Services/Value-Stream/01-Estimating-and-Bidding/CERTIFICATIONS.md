# Estimating & Bidding — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Estimating & Bidding
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **FAR** federal acquisition & sealed-bid/negotiated procurement familiarity
- **Davis-Bacon Act** prevailing-wage determination competency (federal construction) — 40 U.S.C. 3141
- SBA **8(a)** program compliance awareness (sole-source/competitive rules)
- Buy American Act / material-source compliance familiarity
- Bonding & insurance (bid/performance/payment bond) requirements

## Recommended
- AACE **CEP** — Certified Estimating Professional
- ASPE **CPE** — Certified Professional Estimator
- Estimating/takeoff software certification (e.g., Bluebeam, RSMeans)

## Nice-to-have
- LEED Green Associate
- CMAA **CCM** — Certified Construction Manager
- Value engineering (SAVE) training
