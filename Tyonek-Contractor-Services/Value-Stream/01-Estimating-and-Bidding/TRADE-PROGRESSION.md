## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Construction Cost Estimator
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-1051.00 — Cost Estimators
- **Related occupation(s):** 11-9021 Construction Managers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Estimator apprenticeships exist); credential-based
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-1051.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based (Emerging)

## Progression Path
1. **Junior Estimator** — quantity takeoff, subcontractor solicitation, bid assembly
2. **Estimator (CPE/CEP)** — full estimates, pricing strategy, Davis-Bacon/prevailing-wage compliance
3. **Senior / Chief Estimator** — bid strategy, risk pricing, mentors estimators

## Credential Ladder
- Entry: takeoff/estimating software → Mid: ASPE CPE or AACE CEP → Senior: chief-estimator experience
- See `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- FAR + Davis-Bacon are required RTI topics; 8(a) pricing/sole-source rules apply to bid strategy.
