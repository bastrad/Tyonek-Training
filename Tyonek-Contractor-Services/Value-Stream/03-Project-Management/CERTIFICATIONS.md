# Project Management — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Project Management (Execution)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **OSHA 30-Hour Construction** — 29 CFR 1926
- **EM 385-1-1** (USACE Safety & Health) — required on Corps of Engineers / federal projects
- **CQM-C** — Construction Quality Management for Contractors (USACE/NAVFAC) — required to run federal three-phase QC
- Davis-Bacon / certified-payroll administration
- FAR construction clauses, submittals, CPARS familiarity

## Recommended
- **PMP** — Project Management Professional
- CMAA **CCM** — Certified Construction Manager
- Primavera P6 / scheduling certification
- Contractor's license (Alaska GC) qualifying-party familiarity

## Nice-to-have
- LEED AP
- DBIA certification
- Lean construction (LCI) training
