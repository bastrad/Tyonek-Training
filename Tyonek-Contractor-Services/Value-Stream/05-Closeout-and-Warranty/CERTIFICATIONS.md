# Closeout & Warranty — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Closeout & Warranty
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- Federal contract closeout & final-payment procedure familiarity (FAR Part 4 / 52.204)
- As-built drawing & O&M manual documentation standards
- Warranty administration per contract terms
- Records retention requirements (FAR)

## Recommended
- CMAA **CCM** — Certified Construction Manager
- **PMP**
- Construction records / document control certification

## Nice-to-have
- LEED commissioning / building commissioning (CxA)
- Facilities management (IFMA FMP)
- Punch-list / QA management software certification
