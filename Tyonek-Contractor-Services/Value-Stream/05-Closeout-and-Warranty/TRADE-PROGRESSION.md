## Progression Path
1. **Closeout Coordinator** — as-builts, O&M manuals, punch-list tracking, submittal completion
2. **Project Manager (Closeout)** — final payment, warranty administration, records retention
3. **Senior PM / Contracts Lead (CCM/PMP)** — closeout process ownership, mentors coordinators
