# Preconstruction & Design-Build — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Preconstruction & Design-Build
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **Alaska** general/mechanical contractor licensing & registration knowledge (AS 08.18)
- Building & life-safety code familiarity (**IBC / IFC**), local AHJ requirements
- **ADA** / Architectural Barriers Act accessibility compliance (federal facilities)
- Federal design-build (FAR Part 36) contracting familiarity

## Recommended
- **DBIA** — Design-Build Institute of America certification
- **LEED AP** (BD+C)
- CMAA **CCM** — Certified Construction Manager
- VDC / BIM coordination certification

## Nice-to-have
- PE / architect coordination experience
- Envision Sustainability Professional (ENV SP)
- Pull-planning / Lean construction (LCI) training
