# Procurement — Trade Classification & Progression

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Procurement
**Last updated:**

> Maps this job scope to recognized classifications. Business occupation with a credential-based
> ladder rather than a classic trade apprenticeship.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Construction Purchasing Agent / Buyer
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-1023.00 — Purchasing Agents, Except Wholesale, Retail & Farm Products
- **Related occupation(s):** 13-1081 Logisticians; 11-3061 Purchasing Managers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Procurement/Supply Chain); credential-based
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-1023.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based

## Progression Path
1. **Buyer / Purchasing Assistant** — POs, material expediting, vendor records
2. **Purchasing Agent (CPSM/CFCM)** — sourcing, Buy American/Berry compliance, Davis-Bacon awareness
3. **Procurement Manager** — purchasing strategy, supplier management, mentoring

## Credential Ladder
- Entry: FAR basics → Mid: ISM CPSM, NCMA CFCM → Senior: APICS CPIM, NCMA CPCM
- See `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Buy American Act / Berry Amendment sourcing rules are required RTI for federal construction.
