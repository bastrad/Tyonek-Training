# Subcontractor Management — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Subcontractor Management
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- FAR subcontracting compliance — Small Business Subcontracting Plan (FAR 52.219-9)
- **Davis-Bacon** wage & certified-payroll flow-down
- Prompt Payment Act / SBA 8(a) subcontracting-limit compliance
- Insurance, bonding & lien-waiver administration
- OSHA multi-employer worksite responsibilities

## Recommended
- NCMA **CFCM** — Certified Federal Contracts Manager
- CCA (Construction Contract Administration) / CDT — CSI credentials
- **PMP**

## Nice-to-have
- Mediation / dispute-resolution training
- Procore / subcontractor-management software certification
- Certified Compliance & Ethics Professional (CCEP)
