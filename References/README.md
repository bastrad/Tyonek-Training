# References

A reference library for the Tyonek training program — the governing material that drives
training and certification, plus a directory of official sources for review as a Training Manager.

## What's in here
| File / folder | What it is |
| --- | --- |
| [Training-and-Certification-Links.md](Training-and-Certification-Links.md) | **The link directory** — official websites for every regulation, standard, certification, and apprenticeship resource referenced across this repo. |
| [Governing-Standards-Reference.md](Governing-Standards-Reference.md) | A reference "card" for each governing standard: what it is, who issues it, its training relevance, recurrence, and where to obtain the authoritative text. |
| [public-domain-copies/](public-domain-copies) | Plain-text reference extracts of **public-domain U.S. government regulations** (free to copy). |

## Important — copyright & sourcing boundary
This is a U.S. Government contract environment, and reference integrity matters.

- **Public-domain material (free to copy):** U.S. federal regulations (the CFR), OSHA/FAA/DoD
  publications, and government guidance are public domain. Where useful, actual extracts are
  stored in [`public-domain-copies/`](public-domain-copies), and the official source is linked.
- **Copyrighted standards (NOT copied here):** AS9100, AS9110, ISO 9001, AWS D17.1, IPC
  (J-STD-001, A-610, A-620), and NADCAP checklists are **copyrighted** and sold by their
  publishers (SAE, ISO, AWS, IPC, PRI). Their full text is **not** reproduced in this repo —
  doing so would infringe copyright and could put a licensed copy out of revision control.
  For those, this folder provides a **summary + the official purchase/review link** so you
  always work from a current, licensed copy.
- **Always verify the current revision.** Standards and regulations change. Treat any edition
  or frequency noted here as a pointer, and confirm the live version at the official source
  during requirements verification (`../Program-Management/01-Requirements-Verification-Process.md`).
