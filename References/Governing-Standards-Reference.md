# Governing Standards — Reference Library

A one-card summary of each standard/regulation that drives training in this repo: what it is,
who issues it, its **training relevance**, how often training recurs, and **where to obtain**
the authoritative text. Use with the [Link Directory](Training-and-Certification-Links.md).

> **Copyright:** items marked 🟢 are public-domain (free). Items marked 🔒 are copyrighted and
> must be purchased/licensed from the publisher — their full text is **not** stored in this repo.
> Editions/frequencies are pointers — **verify the current revision** at the source.

### How to use this library
1. During **requirements verification**, open the card for each governing item and confirm the
   current revision at its source link.
2. For copyrighted standards, obtain a **licensed current copy** from the publisher — do not
   rely on summaries or third-party reprints for compliance decisions.
3. Keep this library and the [Link Directory](Training-and-Certification-Links.md) updated as
   standards revise.
