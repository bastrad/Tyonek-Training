# Cable Harness Assembly — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Cable Harness Assembly (Copper & Fiber Optic)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **IPC/WHMA-A-620** Certified IPC Specialist (CIS) — Requirements & Acceptance for Cable & Wire Harness Assemblies (commonly contractually required)
- **IPC J-STD-001** CIS — for soldered wire terminations
- **ANSI/ESD S20.20** ESD control handling
- Fiber optic termination & inspection qualification per customer/**ARINC** specification (facility holds ARINC certification)
- Certified crimp-tool / contact insertion qualification (per connector spec — M22520, etc.)
- Hazard Communication — OSHA 1910.1200

## Recommended
- FOA **CFOT** — Certified Fiber Optic Technician
- IPC-A-620 Certified IPC Trainer (CIT) — for leads
- Wire marking & IPC/WHMA-A-620 wire prep endorsements

## Nice-to-have
- FOA CFOS (specialist: splicing, testing, connectors)
- ARINC 801/802 fiber connector endorsement
- IPC J-STD-001 (cross-training with CCA)
