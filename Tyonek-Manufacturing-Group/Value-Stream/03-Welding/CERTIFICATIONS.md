# Welding — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Welding
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- Welder qualification to the applicable code/process for the job:
  - **AWS D17.1** — Specification for Fusion Welding for Aerospace Applications
  - Customer/OEM weld specifications (AMS, MIL, drawing-called specs)
- NADCAP special-process compliance (welding) — facility accreditation the welder works under
- Hot Work permit / fire watch training — OSHA 1910.252
- Hexavalent chromium & welding fume respiratory protection — OSHA 1910.1026 / 1910.134
- Compressed / shielding gas handling & storage
- Hazard Communication (HazCom / GHS) — OSHA 1910.1200
- PPE qualification (welding, eye, respiratory)

## Recommended
- AWS Certified Welder (CW)
- AWS D1.1 (structural steel), D1.2 (aluminum) per work mix
- AWS Certified Welding Inspector (CWI) — for leads/inspectors
- Brazing qualification (AWS/AMS) if applicable

## Nice-to-have
- AWS Certified Welding Educator (CWE)
- Additional process endorsements (GTAW/GMAW/electron-beam)
- NDT familiarity (VT/PT) — NAS410 / SNT-TC-1A
