# Reverse Engineering & TDP Development — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Reverse Engineering & Tech Data Package Development
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **ITAR / EAR** export-control training — 22 CFR 120–130 / 15 CFR 730–774
- **MIL-STD-31000** Technical Data Package standard competency
- GD&T (ASME Y14.5) competency
- Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) handling
- Data rights / intellectual-property marking awareness (DFARS 252.227-7013/7014)

## Recommended
- 3D scanning & metrology certification (FARO, Creaform, GOM)
- CAD certifications (CATIA / SolidWorks)
- ASME GD&T Professional (GDTP)

## Nice-to-have
- Certified Manufacturing Engineer (CMfgE / SME)
- PMP
- Materials characterization / lab analysis familiarity
