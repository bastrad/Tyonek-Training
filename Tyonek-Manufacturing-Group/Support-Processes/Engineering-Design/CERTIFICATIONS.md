# Engineering & Design — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Engineering & Design (CATIA, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Adobe 3D)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **ITAR / EAR** export-control training — 22 CFR 120–130 / 15 CFR 730–774 (defense technical data)
- GD&T (ASME Y14.5) competency (build-to-print drawing authority)
- Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) handling
- Configuration/data management awareness (drawing release control, AS9100)

## Recommended
- Dassault CATIA certification
- SolidWorks CSWP / CSWE
- Autodesk AutoCAD Certified Professional
- ASME GD&T Professional (GDTP)

## Nice-to-have
- Professional Engineer (PE) license
- Model-Based Definition (MBD) / MBE competency
- PMP (for project/design leads)
