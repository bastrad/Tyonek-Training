# Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.

**Umbrella:** Tyonek
**Sector:** Manufacturing

Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc. companies build OEM-quality mechanical and
electrical assemblies, components, and kits for aviation, missile, and ground
systems. Vertically integrated operations include special processes and
capabilities certified to ISO 9001:2015, AS9100, and OEM quality standards.
Specialty: complex, high-quality electro-mechanical assemblies requiring on-site
testing and validation in accordance with aerospace standards.

## Capabilities
- 5-axis, 4-axis, and 3-axis CNC mills, lathes, and sheet metal cutters (18 machines)
- Water jet and large-format 4-axis CNC
- On-site weld, coatings, paint, markings, and assembly
- On-site circuit card assembly (CCA, SMT)
- Copper and fiber optic cable harness assembly, wire marking, and testing
- Quality lab with CMM, Faro Arm, and laser scan testing
- Reverse engineering and tech data package development
- On-staff engineers in all disciplines
- Complete build-to-print capability
- Extensive supplier management for raw materials and components with
  FAR-compliant estimating, purchasing, and cost accounting systems

## Software
- CATIA
- SolidWorks
- AutoCAD
- Adobe 3D

## Certifications
- ISO 9001:2015
- AS9100
- J-STD, IPC, ARINC (electrical and fiber optics)
- AWS and various OEM certifications (welding)
