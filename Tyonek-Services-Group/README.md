# Tyonek Services Group, Inc.

**Umbrella:** Tyonek
**Sector:** Services (Maintenance, Repair & Overhaul)

Tyonek Services Group, Inc. companies provide aircraft engineering, modification,
maintenance, and testing at Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) facilities
totaling 245,000 sq ft at Huntsville International Airport (HSV) and Stennis
International Airport (HSA). The MRO facilities can induct aircraft as large as
the C-130. Tyonek companies provide on-site maintenance and modification work at
Government-owned facilities across the country, and Contract Field Teams provide
on-call support for scheduled and unscheduled maintenance needs. Facilities and
operations are certified to ISO 9001:2015, AS9100, AS9110, and DCMA 8210.1C
standards, supporting fixed-wing and rotary-wing platforms for all four military
services, U.S. Coast Guard, U.S. Customs and Border Protection, and USDA customers.

## Capabilities
- 5 x C-130 maintenance / modification bays
- 36 x UH-60 or equivalent sized aircraft bays
- DCMA-approved flight / ground operations procedures
- FAA Part 145 Certified Repair Stations
- Tooling and equipment for fixed- and rotary-wing work
- On-site fire departments for NFPA 409 compliance
- Experienced aviation quality inspectors and managers
- Safeguarding and storage for controlled data and material, including AA&E, HAZMAT, and communications equipment
- Engineering support for development and submission of technical work orders, work cards, and ECPs
- Multiple shifts and availability to expand operations
- Integrated supply chain and logistics support
- Field training / New Equipment Training (NET)
- Airworthiness support for testing and validation
- Certified A&P mechanics and test pilots on staff
