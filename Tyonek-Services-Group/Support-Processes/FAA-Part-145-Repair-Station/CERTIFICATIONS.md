# FAA Part 145 Repair Station Operations — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** FAA Part 145 Certified Repair Station Operations
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **FAA Part 145** Repair Station certificate (facility) — 14 CFR Part 145
- Repair Station & Quality Manual (RSQM) / Operations Specifications training
- FAA **A&P** certificate (mechanics); accountable manager designation (management)
- FAA/DOT Anti-Drug & Alcohol program — 14 CFR Part 120
- Recordkeeping & return-to-service authority training
- **AS9110** aviation maintenance QMS awareness

## Recommended
- FAA **Inspection Authorization (IA)**
- Safety Management System (SMS) training
- Human Factors in maintenance
- Internal auditor (AS9110)

## Nice-to-have
- EASA Part-145 familiarity (foreign work)
- Accountable Manager / regulatory affairs training
- Root Cause / CAPA facilitation
