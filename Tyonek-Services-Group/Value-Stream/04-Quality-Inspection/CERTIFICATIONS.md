# Aviation Quality Inspection — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Aviation Quality Inspection
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- FAA **Airframe & Powerplant (A&P)** certificate (for inspectors)
- **FAA Part 145** inspection procedures / inspector authorization per RSQM
- **RII** (Required Inspection Item) inspector qualification
- **AS9110** aviation maintenance quality-system awareness
- **DCMA 8210.1C** familiarity
- Calibration & measurement traceability awareness — NIST

## Recommended
- FAA **Inspection Authorization (IA)**
- ASQ **CQI** / **CQA** — Certified Quality Inspector / Auditor
- AS9110 Internal Auditor
- **NAS410** NDT certification (Level II)

## Nice-to-have
- ASQ **CQE** — Certified Quality Engineer
- Safety Management System (SMS) auditor
- Root Cause / Corrective Action (RCCA) facilitation
