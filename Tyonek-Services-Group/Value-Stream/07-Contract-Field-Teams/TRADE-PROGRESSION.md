## Trade Classification
- **Primary trade / occupation:** Aircraft Mechanic (Deployed / Field)
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 49-3011.00 — Aircraft Mechanics and Service Technicians
- **Related occupation(s):** 49-2091.00 Avionics Technicians
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Apprenticeable (Aircraft Mechanic)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=49-3011.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** High (same trade; adds deployment readiness)

## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice Mechanic** — bench experience before field assignment
2. **Journeyman A&P Mechanic (Field-Qualified)** — deployable, multi-platform, self-sufficient at customer sites
3. **Master / Team Lead** — leads field teams, multi-platform authority, deployment planning, mentors

## Typical Apprenticeship Structure
- Term: ~8,000 OJL hours (A&P base) + field-readiness qualifications
- Related instruction: platform quals, HAZMAT shipping, force protection, deployment processing
- Completion credential: FAA A&P + field/deployment qualification

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Field readiness is best layered onto the master/journeyman tier — not an entry program.
- Deployment quals (CRC, HAZMAT shipping, AT Level I) from `CERTIFICATIONS.md` gate field eligibility.
