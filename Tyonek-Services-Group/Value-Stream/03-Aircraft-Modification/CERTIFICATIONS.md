# Aircraft Modification — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Aircraft Modification
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- FAA **Airframe & Powerplant (A&P)** certificate — 14 CFR Part 65
- **FAA Part 145** Repair Station procedures
- **DCMA 8210.1C** familiarity
- Modification per approved data (STC / Engineering Orders / Technical Work Orders / ECPs)
- Configuration management & as-modified documentation training
- OSHA 10/30-Hour; HAZMAT handling

## Recommended
- FAA **Inspection Authorization (IA)**
- Sheet-metal / structures repair certification
- DER coordination / approved-data familiarity
- Electrical & wiring (EWIS) modification training

## Nice-to-have
- NDT Level II — NAS410
- Composite repair certification
- Systems integration / avionics installation
