# Rotary-Wing Aircraft Maintenance — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Rotary-Wing Aircraft Maintenance (UH-60)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- FAA **Airframe & Powerplant (A&P)** certificate — 14 CFR Part 65
- **FAA Part 145** Repair Station procedures / RSQM training
- **DCMA 8210.1C** Contractor's Flight & Ground Operations familiarity
- **RII** (Required Inspection Item) qualification
- UH-60 platform-specific maintenance qualification (technical-order driven)
- FAA/DOT Anti-Drug & Alcohol program — 14 CFR Part 120
- OSHA 10/30-Hour; HAZMAT handling
- FOD prevention program

## Recommended
- FAA **Inspection Authorization (IA)**
- Rotor track & balance certification (RADS/vibration)
- Engine run qualification
- Dynamic component / drivetrain training

## Nice-to-have
- NDT Level II — NAS410
- FCC General Radiotelephone
- Composite rotor blade repair
