# Training & Certification — Link Directory

Official sources for reviewing and obtaining the regulations, standards, certifications, and
apprenticeship resources a Tyonek Training Manager needs. Grouped by type.

> Links point to official/authoritative sites. URLs occasionally change — if one moves, search
> the organization's name. Government (.gov) and standards-body sites are the sources of truth.
