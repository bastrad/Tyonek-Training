# Trade & Progression Index

Every Tyonek work process mapped to its recognized **U.S. DOL / O*NET-SOC** skilled-trade
or occupation, with an **apprenticeship-suitability** rating for standing up an
Apprentice → Journeyman → Master progression program.

**Suitability key**
- **High** — established DOL Registered Apprenticeship; register now.
- **Moderate** — technician tier is apprenticeable; professional tier is licensure/credential.
- **Credential-based** — professional/licensure ladder (DOL apprenticeship optional/emerging).
- **N/A** — mission-defined; map to the specific underlying trade.

> Codes are O*NET-SOC. Look up the live DOL listing at
> `apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=<code>`.
> Confirm exact RAPIDS codes and terms at registration.

## At a glance
- **46** work processes mapped across 4 subsidiaries.
- **Register-now apprenticeship targets (High):** CNC/Machinist, Welder, Electronics &
  Harness Assembly, Painter/Coater, QC Inspector, Aircraft Mechanic (A&P) across all MRO
  lines, Firefighter/ARFF, and the Cyber/IT/Software occupations — the strongest candidates
  for a registered Apprentice → Journeyman → Master pipeline.
- **Moderate** roles support an apprenticeable technician tier feeding a licensed/credentialed
  professional tier (drafting, engineering technician, safety, logistics, construction inspection).
- **Credential-based** roles (procurement, security/FSO, accounting, HR, ISD, strategic
  planning, most construction management) progress through certification/licensure ladders.
