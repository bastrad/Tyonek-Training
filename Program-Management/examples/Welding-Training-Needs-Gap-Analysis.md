### What the three routes mean (from this example)
- **Train** (rows 3, 9) — the material already exists; specific people just need to take/retake it.
- **Develop** (rows 11, 12) — **no training material exists yet**, so it must be *built* (ISD/ADDIE).
  This is the "create new training if not already implemented" path.
- **Records** (row 13) — the training is happening, but the *proof* is missing → fix documentation.
