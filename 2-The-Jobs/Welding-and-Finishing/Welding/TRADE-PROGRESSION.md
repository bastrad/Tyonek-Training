## Trade Classification
- **Primary trade / occupation:** Welder (Combination)
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 51-4121.00 — Welders, Cutters, Solderers, and Brazers
- **Related occupation(s):** 51-4122.00 Welding, Soldering & Brazing Machine Setters/Operators
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Apprenticeable (Welder / Welder-Combination)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=51-4121.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** High

## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice Welder** — process fundamentals (GTAW/GMAW), safety, joint prep, tack welding
2. **Journeyman Welder** — code-qualified production welding (e.g., AWS D17.1 aerospace), multi-position
3. **Master Welder / Certified Welding Inspector (CWI)** — inspection authority, procedure (WPS) development, mentor

## Typical Apprenticeship Structure
- Term: ~2,000–6,000 OJL hours (typically 3 years, time-based)
- On-the-job learning + ~144 hrs/yr Related Technical Instruction (RTI)
- Completion credential: journey-level Welder + maintained AWS/OEM weld qualifications
