# Trade & Progression Index

Every Tyonek job mapped to its recognized **U.S. DOL / O*NET-SOC** trade or occupation, with an
**apprenticeship-suitability** rating for standing up an Apprentice → Journeyman → Master program.
Grouped by discipline; the **Company** column shows which subsidiary the job sits in.

**Suitability key**
- **High** — established DOL Registered Apprenticeship; register now.
- **Moderate** — technician tier is apprenticeable; professional tier is licensure/credential.
- **Credential-based** — professional/licensure ladder (DOL apprenticeship optional/emerging).
- **N/A** — mission-defined; map to the specific underlying trade.

> Codes are O*NET-SOC. Look up the live DOL listing at
> `apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=<code>`.

## At a glance
- **46** jobs mapped across **12 disciplines** and 4 companies.
- **Register-now apprenticeship targets (High):** CNC/Machinist, Welder, Electronics & Harness
  Assembly, Painter/Coater, QC Inspector, Aircraft Mechanic (A&P) across all aviation lines,
  Firefighter/ARFF, and the Cyber/IT/Software occupations — the strongest candidates for a
  registered Apprentice → Journeyman → Master pipeline.
- **Moderate** roles support an apprenticeable technician tier feeding a licensed/credentialed
  professional tier (drafting, engineering technician, safety, logistics, construction inspection).
- **Credential-based** roles (procurement, security/FSO, accounting, HR, ISD, strategic planning,
  most construction management) progress through certification/licensure ladders.
