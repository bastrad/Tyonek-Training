**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Strategic Planning
**Last updated:**

> Maps this job scope to recognized classifications. Professional/analyst occupation with a
> credential-based ladder rather than a classic trade apprenticeship.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Management Analyst / Project Management Specialist
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-1111.00 — Management Analysts
- **Related occupation(s):** 13-1082.00 Project Management Specialists
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Project Management apprenticeships exist)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-1082.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based (Emerging)

## Progression Path
1. **Analyst / Planner** — data gathering, plan support, JOPES/APEX familiarity
2. **Senior Planner / Project Manager (PMP)** — leads planning efforts, risk management
3. **Program Manager (PgMP) / Strategy Lead** — portfolio strategy, mentors analysts

## Credential Ladder (in lieu of trade journey levels)
- Entry: analyst experience → Mid: PMP → Senior: PgMP, Lean Six Sigma
- Clearance per contract; see `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Run as a certification-based career ladder; PM apprenticeship is optional and competency-based.
