# Recruiting & Staffing — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Recruiting & Staffing (Hard-to-Fill Positions)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **OFCCP** compliance — Affirmative Action / EEO obligations for federal contractors (41 CFR 60)
- OFCCP-compliant applicant tracking & recordkeeping
- Personnel security / clearance eligibility process familiarity (e-QIP/SF-86, JPAS/DISS awareness)
- Data privacy (PII) handling

## Recommended
- SHRM-CP / HRCI **PHR**
- AIRS Certified Diversity & Inclusion Recruiter (CDR)
- Technical / cleared-recruiting certification

## Nice-to-have
- SHRM-SCP / HRCI SPHR
- LinkedIn Recruiter certification
- Compensation (CCP) familiarity
