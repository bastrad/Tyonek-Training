> with a credential-based ladder; DOL HR apprenticeships are emerging.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Human Resources / Recruiting Specialist
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-1071.00 — Human Resources Specialists
- **Related occupation(s):** 13-1141 Compensation/Benefits; 11-3121 HR Managers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Human Resources apprenticeships exist)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-1071.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based (Emerging)

## Progression Path
1. **Recruiting Coordinator / Sourcer** — sourcing, screening, OFCCP-compliant recordkeeping
2. **Recruiter / HR Specialist (SHRM-CP/PHR)** — full-cycle cleared recruiting, offer management
3. **Senior / Talent Acquisition Lead (SHRM-SCP)** — strategy, program ownership, mentors recruiters

## Credential Ladder
- Entry: OFCCP/EEO training → Mid: SHRM-CP or HRCI PHR, AIRS CDR → Senior: SHRM-SCP / SPHR
- See `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- OFCCP compliance (federal contractor) is a required RTI topic regardless of ladder chosen.
