## Trade Classification
- **Primary trade / occupation:** Design Drafter / Metrology Technician (trade); Engineer (professional)
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 17-3013.00 — Mechanical Drafters
- **Related occupation(s):** 17-3028.00 Calibration Technologists & Technicians (3D scanning/metrology); 17-2141.00 Mechanical Engineers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Apprenticeable as **Drafter / Engineering Technician**
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=17-3013.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Moderate

## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice / Junior Drafter-Scanner** — 3D scanning, point-cloud to CAD, drawing standards
2. **Journeyman Design Drafter** — full TDP development to MIL-STD-31000, GD&T, data-rights marking
3. **Lead / Engineer / Master** — RE strategy, materials analysis coordination, TDP validation

## Typical Structure
- Drafter apprenticeship term: ~4,000–8,000 OJL hours + ~144 hrs/yr RTI
- Completion credential: journey-level Drafter/Designer
