# Engineering Support (TWOs, Work Cards, ECPs) — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Engineering Support (Technical Work Orders, Work Cards, ECPs)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **ITAR / EAR** export-control training
- Configuration Management training (ECP/engineering-change control — EIA-649)
- Technical data / tech-manual standards (MIL-STD-38784, S1000D) familiarity
- Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) handling

## Recommended
- FAA DER / ODA appointment (for approved data)
- Professional Engineer (PE) license
- CMPIC / configuration management certification
- Technical writing certification

## Nice-to-have
- PMP
- INCOSE CSEP (systems engineering)
- Reliability engineering (CRE)
