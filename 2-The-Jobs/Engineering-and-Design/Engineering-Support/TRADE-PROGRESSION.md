## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice / Junior Tech** — work-card/TWO drafting, config-mgmt basics, tech-data standards
2. **Journeyman Engineering Technician / Tech Writer** — authors ECPs & work instructions to MIL-STD/S1000D
3. **Lead / Engineer (PE) / DER** — approved-data authority, configuration control ownership, mentoring

## Typical Structure
- Engineering Technician apprenticeship (~4,000–8,000 OJL hrs + RTI)
- Engineer/DER tier is degree + FAA/DER appointment (not apprenticeship)
