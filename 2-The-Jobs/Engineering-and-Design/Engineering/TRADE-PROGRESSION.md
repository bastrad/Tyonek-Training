# Engineering — Trade Classification & Progression

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Engineering
**Last updated:**

> Maps this job scope to recognized classifications. Professional engineering follows a degree +
> licensure ladder; the technician tier is apprenticeable.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Engineer (discipline varies); Engineering Technician (technician tier)
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 17-2199.00 — Engineers, All Other (discipline-dependent); 17-3029 Engineering Technicians
- **Related occupation(s):** 17-2112 Industrial Engineers; 15-1299 Computer/Systems roles
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Apprenticeable at the **Engineering Technician** tier; engineer tier is licensure
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=17-3029.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Moderate

## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice / Engineering Technician** — supports design/analysis, tooling, documentation
2. **Journey Engineering Technician / Engineer (FE/EIT)** — independent technical work
3. **Professional Engineer (PE) / Lead / Master** — design authority, systems engineering (CSEP), mentoring

## Typical Structure
- Technician tier: apprenticeship (~4,000–8,000 OJL hrs + RTI)
- Engineer tier: ABET degree → FE/EIT → PE (not apprenticeship)

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Register the technician tier; ITAR and discipline competency from `CERTIFICATIONS.md` are core RTI.
