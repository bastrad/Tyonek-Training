# Waterjet & Large-Format Cutting — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Waterjet & Large-Format 4-Axis Cutting
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- OSHA 10-Hour General Industry (shop floor safety) — OSHA 29 CFR 1910
- Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) qualification — OSHA 1910.147
- Machine guarding & hazardous energy training
- High-pressure equipment / intensifier pump safety (manufacturer-specific)
- Respirable crystalline silica awareness (abrasive garnet handling) — OSHA 1910.1053
- Hazard Communication (HazCom / GHS) — OSHA 1910.1200
- PPE qualification (eye, hearing, hand)
- Internal operator qualification / machine sign-off (AS9100 work-instruction control)

## Recommended
- Waterjet OEM operator certification (OMAX, Flow, etc.)
- NIMS Machining Level I (Measurement, Materials & Safety)
- Blueprint reading & GD&T (ASME Y14.5) fundamentals
- Overhead crane / rigging & sling certification (large-format material handling)

## Nice-to-have
- CAD/CAM nesting software certification
- Forklift operator certification — OSHA 1910.178
- OSHA 30-Hour General Industry
