## Trade Classification
- **Primary trade / occupation:** Machinist (CNC)
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 51-4041.00 — Machinists
- **Related occupation(s):** 51-9161.00 CNC Tool Operators; 51-9162.00 CNC Tool Programmers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Apprenticeable (Machinist is a classic apprenticeable trade)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=51-4041.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** High

## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice Machinist** — machine setup, tooling, measurement, blueprint/GD&T fundamentals
2. **Journeyman Machinist** — independent multi-axis setup & operation, close-tolerance work, first article
3. **Master Machinist / CNC Programmer / Lead** — CAM programming, process development, OJT mentor

## Typical Apprenticeship Structure
- Term: ~8,000 OJL hours (4 years, time-based) — classic machinist term
- On-the-job learning + ~144 hrs/yr Related Technical Instruction (RTI)
- Completion credential: journey-level Machinist

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Excellent anchor trade for a Registered Apprenticeship; pairs with NIMS stackable credentials as RTI.
- Journey-out can align with NIMS CNC Milling/Turning and GD&T from `CERTIFICATIONS.md`.
