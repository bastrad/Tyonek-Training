# CNC Machining — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** CNC Machining (5/4/3-Axis Mills, Lathes)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- OSHA 10-Hour General Industry — OSHA 29 CFR 1910
- Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) qualification — OSHA 1910.147
- Machine guarding & hazardous energy training
- Hazard Communication (HazCom / GHS) — OSHA 1910.1200
- Metalworking fluid / coolant handling & PPE
- Internal machine operator qualification & first-article sign-off (AS9100 work-instruction control)

## Recommended
- NIMS CNC Milling / CNC Turning: Operations & Programming Setup
- NIMS CNC Machining Level I & II
- GD&T (ASME Y14.5) competency
- CAM software certification (Mastercam, Fusion 360, etc.)
- Precision measurement & metrology (micrometers, calipers, gages)

## Nice-to-have
- SolidWorks CSWA / CSWP
- OSHA 30-Hour General Industry
- Overhead crane / rigging certification
