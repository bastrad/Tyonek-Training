# Circuit Card Assembly (CCA / SMT) — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Circuit Card Assembly (CCA, SMT)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **IPC J-STD-001** Certified IPC Specialist (CIS) — Requirements for Soldered Electrical & Electronic Assemblies (commonly contractually required for DoD/aerospace)
- **IPC-A-610** CIS — Acceptability of Electronic Assemblies
- **ANSI/ESD S20.20** ESD control program awareness/handling
- J-STD-001 Space Addendum — *Required* when the contract is space/satellite hardware
- Hazard Communication (solder, flux, solvents) — OSHA 1910.1200
- Lead/solder exposure & hygiene program — OSHA 1910.1025

## Recommended
- IPC-7711/7721 CIS — Rework, Modification & Repair
- IPC-A-600 CIS — Acceptability of Printed Boards
- Certified IPC Trainer (CIT) — for leads/trainers
- Soldering endorsement per J-STD-001 (hand soldering, SMT, through-hole)

## Nice-to-have
- IPC-6012 (rigid board qualification) familiarity
- IPC-WHMA-A-620 (cross-training with harness)
- Six Sigma / lean electronics
