## Trade Classification
- **Procurement — occupation:** Purchasing Agent — **O*NET 13-1023.00** (credential-based)
- **Kitting/Fabrication — trade:** Assembler — **O*NET 51-2092.00** Team Assemblers (apprenticeable)
- **Related occupation(s):** 13-1081 Logisticians; 43-5111 Weighers/Checkers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Apprenticeable for the assembler/kitting tier; procurement is credential-based
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=51-2092.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Moderate (split by function)

## Progression Path
- **Kitting/fabrication:** Apprentice Assembler → Journey Kitter/Fabricator → Lead
- **Procurement:** Buyer → Purchasing Agent (CFCM/CPSM) → Procurement Lead

## Typical Structure
- Assembler apprenticeship (~2,000–4,000 OJL hrs + RTI) for the kitting tier
- Procurement credential ladder (FAR/DFARS → CFCM/CPSM)
