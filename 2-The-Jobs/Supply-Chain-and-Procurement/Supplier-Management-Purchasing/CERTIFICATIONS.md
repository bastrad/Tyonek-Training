# Supplier Management & Purchasing — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Supplier Management, Estimating & Purchasing
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **FAR / DFARS** procurement compliance training
- Contractor Purchasing System Review (**CPSR**) requirements awareness
- **AS5553 / AS6081** Counterfeit Electronic Parts avoidance (DoD requirement)
- **ITAR / EAR** export-control training
- FAR-compliant estimating & cost/price analysis awareness (DFARS 252.215-7002)

## Recommended
- NCMA **CFCM** — Certified Federal Contracts Manager
- ISM **CPSM** — Certified Professional in Supply Management
- APICS **CPIM / CSCP** — production & supply chain

## Nice-to-have
- NCMA CPCM — Certified Professional Contracts Manager
- Six Sigma / lean supply chain
- Certified Purchasing Manager (CPM)
