# Supplier Management & Purchasing — Trade Classification & Progression

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Supplier Management, Estimating & Purchasing
**Last updated:**

> Maps this job scope to the recognized U.S. DOL classification and its progression. This is a
> business occupation with a credential-based ladder rather than a classic construction-style trade.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary trade / occupation:** Purchasing Agent / Procurement Specialist
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-1023.00 — Purchasing Agents, Except Wholesale, Retail & Farm Products
- **Related occupation(s):** 13-1081.00 Logisticians; 11-3061.00 Purchasing Managers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Supply Chain / Procurement apprenticeships exist but are not a traditional trade)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-1023.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based

## Progression Path (credential-based, not apprentice/journeyman/master)
1. **Buyer / Procurement Assistant** — POs, expediting, supplier records, FAR/DFARS basics
2. **Purchasing Agent / Subcontract Administrator** — sourcing, cost/price analysis, CPSR compliance
3. **Procurement Lead / Manager (CFCM/CPSM)** — supplier strategy, negotiations, purchasing-system ownership

## Credential Ladder (in lieu of trade journey levels)
- Entry: FAR/DFARS training → Mid: NCMA CFCM / ISM CPSM → Senior: NCMA CPCM / APICS CSCP
- See `CERTIFICATIONS.md` for the full list.

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Consider a **non-trade (competency-based) apprenticeship** in Supply Chain / Procurement if you want a registered pathway; otherwise run this as a certification-based career ladder.
