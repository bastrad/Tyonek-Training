# Integrated Supply Chain & Logistics — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Integrated Supply Chain & Logistics Support
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **DOT / IATA / IMDG HAZMAT (Dangerous Goods) shipping** certification — 49 CFR 172.700 (legally required to ship hazmat)
- **Government Property Management** — FAR 52.245-1 / FAR Part 45
- FAR / DFARS awareness
- Counterfeit parts avoidance (AS5553/AS6081) for procured material
- ITAR/EAR awareness (controlled item movement)

## Recommended
- NPMA **CPPM** — Certified Professional Property Manager
- APICS **CPIM / CSCP** — planning & supply chain
- DLA / DLMS / GFP-module (government-furnished property) training

## Nice-to-have
- Lean / Six Sigma logistics
- Forklift & materials-handling equipment certification
- Warehouse management system (WMS) certification
