# Integrated Supply Chain & Logistics — Trade Classification & Progression

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Integrated Supply Chain & Logistics Support
**Last updated:**

> Maps this job scope to recognized classifications. Frontline material handling is
> apprenticeable; logistician/planner roles follow a credential-based ladder.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Logistician / Logistics Technician
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-1081.00 — Logisticians
- **Related occupation(s):** 43-5061.00 Production, Planning & Expediting Clerks; 43-5071.00 Shipping/Receiving; 53-7062 Material Movers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Supply Chain / Logistics apprenticeships exist)
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-1081.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Moderate

## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice / Material Handler** — receiving, kitting, property records, HAZMAT-shipping basics
2. **Journeyman Logistics Technician** — inventory control, GFP management, DLMS transactions
3. **Logistician / Property Manager (CPPM) / Lead** — supply-chain planning, program property accountability

## Typical Structure
- Logistics apprenticeship (~2,000–4,000 OJL hrs + RTI) for the technician tier
- Credential ladder: NPMA CPPM, APICS CPIM/CSCP

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- DOT/IATA HAZMAT shipping and government-property (FAR 52.245-1) from `CERTIFICATIONS.md` are required RTI.
