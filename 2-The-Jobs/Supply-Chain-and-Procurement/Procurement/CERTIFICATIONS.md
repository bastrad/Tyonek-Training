# Procurement — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Procurement
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **FAR / DFARS** procurement compliance
- **Buy American Act** / **Berry Amendment** material-sourcing compliance (federal construction)
- **Davis-Bacon** applicability to on-site fabricated/delivered labor
- Government property / GFP handling — FAR 52.245-1

## Recommended
- ISM **CPSM** — Certified Professional in Supply Management
- NCMA **CFCM / CPCM**
- APICS **CPIM**

## Nice-to-have
- Lean / Six Sigma
- Construction procurement software certification
- Certified Purchasing Manager (CPM)
