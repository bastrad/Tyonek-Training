# Fire & Emergency Services — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Fire & Emergency Services (NFPA 409 Compliance)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **NFPA 1001** Firefighter I / II certification
- **NFPA 1003** Airport/Aircraft Rescue Fire Fighting (ARFF)
- HAZMAT Awareness & Operations — OSHA 1910.120 (HAZWOPER) / NFPA 470
- CPR / First Aid / AED certification
- **NFPA 409** aircraft-hangar fire-protection systems familiarity (AFFF/foam suppression)
- Respiratory protection & SCBA — OSHA 1910.134

## Recommended
- **EMT-Basic** (state/NREMT)
- NFPA 1021 Fire Officer I
- Confined space / technical rescue
- Fire apparatus driver/operator (NFPA 1002)

## Nice-to-have
- Paramedic certification
- NFPA 1031 Fire Inspector
- NFPA 1041 Fire Instructor
