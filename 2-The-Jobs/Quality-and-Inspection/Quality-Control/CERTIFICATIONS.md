# Quality Control — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Contractor Services, LLC
**Work Process:** Quality Control
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **CQM-C** — Construction Quality Management for Contractors (USACE/NAVFAC) — mandatory for the QC Manager on federal projects
- Three-Phase Control System (Preparatory / Initial / Follow-up) competency
- **EM 385-1-1** safety-quality interface familiarity
- Submittal register & RFI management; definable features of work (DFOW)

## Recommended
- **ACI** Concrete Field Testing Technician Grade I
- AWS **CWI** — Certified Welding Inspector (structural work)
- **ICC** inspector certifications (building/structural)
- **NICET** (civil/geotechnical/materials)

## Nice-to-have
- ASQ **CQE** — Certified Quality Engineer
- Lean Six Sigma
- Commissioning (CxA) familiarity
