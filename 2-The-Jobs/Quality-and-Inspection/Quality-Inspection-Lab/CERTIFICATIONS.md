# Quality Inspection Lab — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Manufacturing Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Quality Inspection Lab (CMM, Faro Arm, Laser Scan)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- GD&T (ASME Y14.5) competency
- CMM programming & operation qualification (PC-DMIS, Calypso, or equivalent)
- FARO Arm / laser scanner operator certification (OEM)
- **AS9100 / AS9102** First Article Inspection (FAI) reporting competency
- Calibration & measurement traceability awareness — ISO/IEC 17025 / NIST
- Internal inspection stamp/authority qualification (per Quality Manual)

## Recommended
- ASQ **CQI** — Certified Quality Inspector
- ASQ **CQT** — Certified Quality Technician
- AS9100 / AS9101 Internal Auditor
- NADCAP awareness (special-process audits)

## Nice-to-have
- ASQ **CQE** — Certified Quality Engineer
- ASQ **CQA** — Certified Quality Auditor
- Six Sigma Green/Black Belt
- NDT Level II (VT/PT/MT) — NAS410 / SNT-TC-1A
