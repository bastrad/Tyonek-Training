# Contract Field Teams — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Contract Field Teams (On-Call Scheduled & Unscheduled Support)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- FAA **A&P** certificate
- Platform-specific maintenance qualification(s) for the assigned aircraft
- **DCMA 8210.1C** familiarity
- Security clearance (as required by task order)
- Deployment readiness — medical, CRC/CONUS Replacement Center processing, immunizations (CONUS/OCONUS)
- DOT/IATA HAZMAT shipping awareness (field material movement)
- OSHA 10/30-Hour

## Recommended
- Multiple platform qualifications (fixed- & rotary-wing)
- Field/flight-line maintenance & GSE qualification
- Anti-terrorism / force protection (Level I)
- Passport / SOFA documentation readiness

## Nice-to-have
- Instructor / on-the-job-training (OJT) qualification
- NDT Level II
- Additional language proficiency
