# Airworthiness Support, Testing & Validation — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Airworthiness Support, Testing & Validation
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **DCMA 8210.1C** familiarity
- **MIL-HDBK-516** Airworthiness Certification Criteria awareness (military platforms)
- FAA **A&P** certificate
- Functional Check Flight (FCF) / ground-test procedure qualification
- Airworthiness release / documentation authority training (per program)

## Recommended
- Test pilot / flight test engineer school
- INCOSE systems engineering (CSEP)
- FAA DER / ODA familiarity
- Data acquisition & instrumentation training

## Nice-to-have
- Airworthiness authority (AWB/TAA) program certification
- Reliability & maintainability (R&M) training
- Statistical / DOE test design
