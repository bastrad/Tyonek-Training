# Flight & Ground Operations — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Flight & Ground Operations (DCMA-Approved Procedures)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- **DCMA 8210.1C** Contractor's Flight & Ground Operations — qualification to the approved procedures (the governing requirement)
- Engine run-up / taxi qualification (ground operators)
- Test/functional-check pilot qualification (for pilots per DCMA 8210.1C)
- Airfield / flight-line driving & movement-area authorization
- Ramp & flight-line safety; hearing conservation — OSHA 1910.95
- FOD prevention program

## Recommended
- FAA Commercial / ATP certificate (for pilots)
- Crew Resource Management (CRM) / human factors
- Ground support equipment (GSE) operation qualification
- Aircraft towing & marshalling certification

## Nice-to-have
- Additional aircraft type ratings
- SETP (Society of Experimental Test Pilots) membership
- Aviation safety officer training
