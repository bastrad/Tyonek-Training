# Training & Exercise Support — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Mission Support (Tyonek Services Group, Inc.)
**Work Process:** Training & Exercise Support
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- Security clearance (level per task order — often TS/SCI for USSOCOM/USAF)
- Formal Instructor / facilitator certification
- Range & exercise safety qualification (live/field events)
- CUI handling; OPSEC training

## Recommended
- Master Training Specialist (MTS)
- Joint exercise planning (JMSEL / MSEL development) training
- Modeling & Simulation (M&S) support familiarity
- CompTIA CTT+

## Nice-to-have
- Joint Professional Military Education (JPME) familiarity
- Project Management Professional (PMP)
- Foreign-language proficiency
