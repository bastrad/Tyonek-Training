# Field Training / New Equipment Training (NET) — Trade Classification & Progression

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Field Training / New Equipment Training (NET)
**Last updated:**

> Maps this job scope to recognized classifications. This is an instructor/trainer occupation
> with a credential-based ladder; it typically builds on an underlying technical trade.

## Trade Classification
- **Primary occupation:** Technical Trainer / Training & Development Specialist
- **O*NET-SOC code:** 13-1151.00 — Training and Development Specialists
- **Related occupation(s):** 25-9031.00 Instructional Coordinators; 49-3011.00 Aircraft Mechanic (SME base)
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship:** Emerging (Instructor/Trainer apprenticeships exist); often credential-based
- **Occupation finder:** https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-occupations/listings?occupationCode=13-1151.00
- **Apprenticeship suitability:** Credential-based (with a technical-trade prerequisite)

## Progression Path
1. **Apprentice / Assistant Instructor** — co-teaches, learns platform SME + instructional basics (ADDIE)
2. **Journeyman / Certified Technical Trainer** — delivers NET independently, develops lesson materials
3. **Master Training Specialist / Lead Instructor** — course ownership, trains instructors, evaluation (Kirkpatrick)

## Typical Structure
- Credential ladder: instructor certification → CompTIA CTT+ → Master Training Specialist
- Prerequisite: journey-level qualification in the technical trade being taught

## Company Sponsorship Notes
- Best run as an instructor-development ladder layered onto the maintenance trades, not a standalone entry apprenticeship.
