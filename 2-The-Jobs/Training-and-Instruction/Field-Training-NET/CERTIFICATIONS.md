# Field Training / New Equipment Training (NET) — Certifications & Qualifications

**Subsidiary:** Tyonek Services Group, Inc.
**Work Process:** Field Training / New Equipment Training (NET)
**Last updated:**

> **Legend** — This is a U.S. Government contract environment. All regulatory, statutory, and contractually-flowed requirements are listed as **Required**.
> - **Required** — mandated by law, regulation, contract, or needed to legally/safely perform the work.
> - **Recommended** — industry standard; strengthens quality, compliance, and competitiveness.
> - **Nice-to-have** — broadens capability or supports career development.

## Required
- Formal Instructor certification (e.g., Army Basic Instructor Course or contract-specified equivalent)
- Platform / equipment technical qualification (A&P or SME on the trained system)
- ADDIE / Instructional Systems Design (ISD) familiarity
- Security clearance (if classified material)
- Range / equipment safety qualification (as applicable)

## Recommended
- CompTIA **CTT+** — Certified Technical Trainer
- ATD **APTD / CPTD** — training & development credential
- Master Training Specialist (MTS)
- Kirkpatrick evaluation methodology

## Nice-to-have
- M.Ed / Instructional Design degree
- eLearning authoring (Articulate/Captivate) certification
- Facilitation / adult-learning certification
